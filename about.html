About Us

Felicific is a Techno-Cultural Festival of Dharamsinh Desai University


In 2013, all branches of FOT & MIS grouped together under a single roof and the journey began. Felicific 2K19 will be a national level techno cultural fest and will be held from 11 to 15 of the March.

Felicific is a journey which began few years ago. With each passing year it grew and evolved and the abstract nothingness was molded into a definite shape with the purpose in the mind. Years of tireless effort and dedicated endeavours, turned this into a legacy which has been proudly carried by legions of students.

The purpose and the core ethics have remained static since the fest's inception. To organize a fest with competitions designed to extract the best from students. Create an aura of aggressiveness contributed in creating frenzy hitherto unseen. But all this will pale in comparison to Felicific 2k19. Bigger,Better and Glitzier, Felicific 2k19 will be all that previous version was much more. With many new competitions and the existing ones updated in sync with technologies, this promises to be the ultimate battle of wits and as the Felicific saga continues and with each step we soar to new heights, we remain grounded to our core values. So why worry about the destination when the journey can be so invigorating? Be a part of Felicific and experience the awesome, spectacular, sublime, enthralling and everything you ever demand of.
