About Us

Six years ago, all the branches of Faculty of Technology and Faculty of Management and Information Science took up a collective responsibility to uphold the techno-cultural ethos of our University. With an aim to provide a platform for the students to bring out their talent and creativity, Felicific welcomes all the Universities to be a part of this festival.

Felicific is a celebration- a celebration that transforms the educational realm into a place of fun, competition and creativity. Although we all belong to different walks of life, Felicific integrates the students and faculties of the University into a single family. Felicific is an outcome of constant hard work, dedication and perfection.

Felicific 2019 is a fresh chapter to this book, which will add ample of memories to cherish forever. With the theme of Evolution, Felicific is here to explore the horizons of imaginations; to celebrate the festival of creativity. Raising the bars and syncing with modern technology, Felicific 2019 has come up with a number of interesting events. Upholding the principle of ‘Soaring new heights of success and achievements by remaining grounded to the core values’. Be a part of this festival and experience the wonderful journey of five days. Let's come together and set new standards of creativity.

LET FELICIFIC 2019 REVERBERATE FOREVER.
